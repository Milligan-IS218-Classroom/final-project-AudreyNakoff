My Life

Childhood

I was born in Colorado Springs, Colorado on June 20th, 2002 to Jennifer and William Johns. My mother was a highschool math teacher and my father was enlisted in the United States Air Force. Shortly after I was born, my dad was deployed for 6 months to South Korea. And so, my mother and I moved to Texas to be closer to her parents while he was away.

It was while we were in Texas that the first of my two biological brothers was born on October 27th, 2003. After my dad returned from Korea, we moved to Spokeskan, Washington. While living there, my second brother was born on September 22, 2005. We moved in 2008 from Washington to the United Kingdom, where we lived for 4 years, returning to the states in 2012.

While in England, my brothers and I attended a British public school. Because of that, I had a very strong British accent when we moved back to the U.S.
