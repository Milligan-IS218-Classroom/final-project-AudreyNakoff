My Favorite Anime

In recent years, I have really gotten into Japanese animated series, called anime, and the comic forms, called manga. Here is a table of some of my favorites, what form of media it is, a short synopsis, and where to read or watch each one.

Title Author Media Type Synopsis Where to Watch/Read
That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Fuse Manga and Anime Lonely thirty-seven-year-old Mikami Satoru is stuck in a dead-end job, unhappy with his mundane life, but after dying at the hands of a robber, he awakens to a fresh start in a fantasy realm...as a slime monster. Watch Seasons 1 & 2 part 1 (there is currently a mid season break) on Vrv
The Slime Diaries Fuse Manga and Anime Originally written as a gag-manga, this is a spin-off, slice-of-life comedy series that follows the events of That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime in which you get to see the everyday life of the characters from the original series. Watch the currently airing season 1 on Vrv
KONOSUBA -God's blessing on this wonderful world! Natsume Akatsuki Manga and Anime The series follows Kazuma Satō, a boy who is sent to a fantasy world with MMORPG elements following his death, where he forms a dysfunctional party with a goddess, an archwizard, and a crusader. Watch seasons 1 & 2 and the movie on Vrv
Spy X Family Tatsuya Endo Manga The story follows a spy who has to "build a family" in order to execute a mission, not realizing that the girl he adopts as a daughter and the woman he agrees to be in a fake marriage with are a mind reader and an assassin. Read this charming story on Viz Media.
Dr. Stone Riichiro Inagaki Manga and Anime Awakened into a world where humanity has been petrified, scientific genius Senku and his brawny friend Taiju use their skills to try to rebuild civilization. Watch seasons 1 & 2 on Vrv
Moriarty the Patriot Ryōsuke Takeuchi Manga and Anime During the late 19th century, Great Britain has become the greatest empire the world has ever known. Hidden within its success, the nation's rigid economic hierarchy dictates the value of one's life solely on status and wealth. To no surprise, the system favors the aristocracy at the top and renders it impossible for the working class to ascend the ranks. William James Moriarty, the second son of the Moriarty household, lives as a regular noble while also being a consultant for the common folk to give them a hand and solve their problems. However, deep inside him lies a desire to destroy the current structure that dominates British society and those who benefit from it. Alongside his brothers Albert and Louis, the trio will do anything it takes to change the filthy world they live in—even if blood must be spilled Watch season 1 & 2, which is currently airing, on FUnimation
